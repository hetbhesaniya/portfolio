# Photo Gallery Directory

Add your personal photos here! 

## Photo Requirements:
- **File names**: `story1.jpg`, `story2.jpg`, `story3.jpg`, `story4.jpg`, `story5.jpg`, `story6.jpg`
- **Recommended size**: 1200x800px or similar landscape ratio
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File size**: Under 500KB per image for optimal loading

## Photo Suggestions:
1. `story1.jpg` - Sunrise/nature shot (e.g., Sedona sunrise)
2. `story2.jpg` - Coffee/workspace setting
3. `story3.jpg` - Hiking/mountain views
4. `story4.jpg` - Golden hour/sunset shot
5. `story5.jpg` - Urban/city exploration
6. `story6.jpg` - Nature/wildlife

You can update the titles, locations, captions, and moods in `pages/AboutMe.js` to match your actual photos!
